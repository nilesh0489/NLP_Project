In an astounding, little-reported act of hubris, SenateRepublicans apparently killed a studyby the non-partisanCongressional Research Service when its findings contradicted their favorite economic myth: that giving tax cuts to the rich helps anyone but themselves. After the study found that lower tax rates for the wealthy "do not appear correlated with economic growth" - though they do increase economic disparities- SenateGOP leadersprotested the study's "methodology," tone, and use of terms like "tax cuts for the rich," and removed it frompublic view.
