Apple Inc. (NASDAQ:AAPL) has won today the multitude of patents including two design properties for the page-turning animation noticed in iOS apps such as iBooks, and IP for the third-generation iPad’s full-body case. With the latest version of iOS mobile operating system from Apple (AAPL), iOS 6, the company has a number of first-party apps that bring into play such a “page curling” animation, counting Maps and Calendar for iPad. The animation was most recently displayed prominently in an iPad mini ad that included iBooks.

Apple on Tuesday has also been granted the design patent for the iPad Smart Case first launched next to the third-generation tablet in 2011. Similar to the original Smart Cover, the Smart Case rotates the connected iPad on and off by using magnetic sensors in the iPad that work together with magnets embedded in the top cover, but provides an extra layer of protection for the tablet’s aluminum chassis.
