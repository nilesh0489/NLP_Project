Karl Rove's now-infamous stand-off with his own people during Fox News' election night coverage, has become fodder for plenty of jokes, and "The Simpsons" got their own dig in on Sunday night's episode.

During the show's patented title sequence blackboard gag, Bart was seen to be repeatedly writing "I Will Not Concede The Election Till Karl Rove Gives Me Permission." Ouch. But not surprising as "The Simpsons" has long poked fun at their parent network's news channel.
