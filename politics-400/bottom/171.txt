            The Department of Defense announced today the death of a soldier who was supporting Operation Enduring Freedom. 

           Staff Sgt. Kenneth W. Bennett, 26, of Glendora, Calif., died Nov. 10, in Sperwan Gar, Afghanistan, from injuries sustained when he encountered an improvised explosive device during combat operations. 

            He was assigned to the 53 Ordnance Company (EOD), 3 Ordnance Battalion (EOD), Joint Base Lewis-McChord, Wash. 

            For more information related to this release, the media may contact the I Corps public affairs office at 253-208-0576.
